About Us

Built by learners,
for everyone.

कोडOS started in May 2021 as an edtech initiative — running bootcamps, online coding hackathons, and workshops on Java, Web Development, Cloud, and Python. Over 4 years we've mentored 1,500+ students and organised 25+ technical workshops.

Along the way, we noticed something. The most valuable lessons weren't in our course material — they were in the real interview rooms and career decisions of the people who had done it. So we started recording those conversations.

What began as interview prep for TCS, Capgemini and Accenture has now grown into a full-spectrum career podcast — spanning technology, finance, design, operations, and beyond. Available on Spotify, Google Podcasts, Amazon Music, and YouTube.
